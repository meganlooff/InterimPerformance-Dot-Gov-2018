Goal Leader: Erik Jones, Assistant Deputy Commissioner, Office of Operations

Goal Statement: Improve the customer experience by reducing the Average Speed of Answer on the National 800 Number.1

  • By September 30, 2020, achieve an average speed of answer of 17 minutes.
  • By September 30, 2021, achieve an average speed of answer of 11 minutes.
