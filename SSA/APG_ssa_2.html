Goal Leader: Marianna LaCanfora, Deputy Commissioner, Office of Analytics, Review, and Oversight

Goal Statement: Improve the integrity of the Supplemental Security Income (SSI) program by focusing our efforts on reducing overpayments (Annual Targets Only).

  • By September 30, 2020, achieve an SSI overpayment accuracy rate of 94 percent.
  • By September 30, 2021, achieve an SSI overpayment accuracy rate of 94 percent.
