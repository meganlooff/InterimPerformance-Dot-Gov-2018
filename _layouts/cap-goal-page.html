June 29, 2020

The U.S. Department of the Treasury and General Services Administration (GSA) are formally designated as Quality Service Management Organizations (QSMO)

The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) has announced Treasury will serve as the QSMO for Core Financial Management services. GSA will be the QSMO for Civilian HR Transaction Services, specifically Compensation Management, Work Schedule and Leave Management services.

April 27, 2020

Administration Announces DHS CISA as the designated QSMO for Cybersecurity Services

The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) has designated the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA) as the Quality Service Management Office for Cybersecurity Services, specifically Security Operations Center standardization, Vulnerability Management standardization, and DNS Resolver service.
