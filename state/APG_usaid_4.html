United States Agency for International Development  

{{page.title}}

Goal Leader: Karl Fickenscher, Deputy Assistant Administrator, Bureau for Economic Growth, Education, and Environment

Goal Statement: Expand engagement with the private sector to achieve greater development outcomes. By September 30, 2021, 100 percent of USAID Missions will have a Private-Sector Engagement (PSE) Plan in place that integrates country-specific private-sector engagement approaches into programming and operations and 90 percent of USAID Missions will demonstrate action taken in line with their PSE Plan.
