Goal Leader: Nick Uehlecke, Advisor, Immediate Office of the Secretary

Goal Statement:

Reduce morbidity and mortality associated with end-stage renal disease and increase patient choice by improving access to alternatives to center-based dialysis. Starting from the baseline for the calendar year ending December 31, 2019, by September 30, 2021.

  • Increase by 10 percent the number of new end-stage renal disease patients on home dialysis.
  • Increase by 10 percent the number of kidney transplants performed.
