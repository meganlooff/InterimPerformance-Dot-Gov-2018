Department of Labor  

Worker Safety: Reduce Miner Injuries

Goal Leaders:

David Zatezalo, Assistant Secretary of Labor for Mine Safety and Health
Tim Watkins, Administrator, Mine Safety and Health Enforcement
Roslyn Fontaine, Deputy Director, Standards, Regulations and Variances

Goal Statement: By September 30, 2021, reduce the reportable injury rate associated with powered haulage equipment, the primary cause of miners’ injuries, by four percent per year based on a rolling five-year average per 200,000 hours worked.
